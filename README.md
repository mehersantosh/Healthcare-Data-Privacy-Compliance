# Healthcare Data Privacy & Compliance Playbook

This repository provides practical guidance on implementing
data privacy, security, and governance frameworks in healthcare
organizations, aligned with Indian regulatory requirements.

It is designed for real-world hospital environments where
clinical operations, compliance, and technology must coexist.

## Focus Areas
- Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023
- NABH-aligned IT controls
- Healthcare data governance & access control
- Incident response & breach management
- Operationalizing privacy without disrupting care

## Who this is for
- Hospital IT Heads & CIOs
- Digital Health Leaders
- Compliance & Quality teams
- Healthcare administrators
